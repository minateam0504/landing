This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how Mina, Inc. a. Information Provided by You: Account details, profile data, photos, listings, descriptions, communications, and payment information (processed by Stripe).

c. Third-Party Sources: Identity and payment verification from Stripe; authentication data from login providers (e.g., Google, Apple).

(i) to operate, provide, and improve the Platform; (ii) to process transactions, facilitate pickups, and handle disputes; (iii) to maintain safety, trust, and compliance; (iv) to communicate with you regarding your account and the Platform; (v) to analyze, aggregate, and anonymize data for the development, training, and improvement of artificial intelligence systems, resale intelligence analytics, product features, and marketing initiatives, and (vi) for any other purpose disclosed to you at the time of collection or with your consent

We may share your information:

(a) with other Users to the extent necessary to facilitate transactions; (b) with service providers (e.g., Stripe, hosting, analytics, support) under confidentiality obligations; (c) in connection with a merger, acquisition, or corporate transaction; (d) in response to lawful requests, legal process, or to enforce our policies; and (e) for purposes of aggregated and anonymized data analysis, research, and commercial use

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Requests may be submitted to hello@getmina.app. These rights are subject to applicable legal exceptions and obligations (e.g., record retention).

We retain personal information as long as necessary for business purposes, dispute resolution, trust and safety enforcement, and compliance with legal and regulatory requirements. Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely.

Such processing may involve profiling or automated decision-making.

9. California Consumer Rights

If you are a resident of California, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act ("CPRA"), including the following:

(a) Right to Know: You may request disclosure of (i) the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you; (ii) the categories of sources from which personal information is collected; (iii) the business or commercial purpose for collecting, using, or disclosing personal information; and (iv) the categories of third parties to whom we disclose personal information.

(b) Right to Delete: You may request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (such as compliance with legal obligations, dispute resolution, or trust and safety enforcement).

(c) Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information maintained about you.

(d) Right to Opt Out of Sale/Sharing: Mina does not sell personal information and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA.

(e) Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information: Mina does not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes other than those permitted under the CCPA/CPRA.

(f) Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA/CPRA rights.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@getmina.app. We may require reasonable verification of your identity before fulfilling such requests. Authorized agents may make requests on your behalf subject to proof of authorization.
